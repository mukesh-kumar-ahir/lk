INTRODUCTION


Government Industrial Training Institues(ITIs) in Chhattisgarh play a vital role in equipping youth with industry-relevant technical skills. Established and managed by the Department of Skills Development. Technical Education and Employment, these institutes aim to provide vocational education and hands-on training to enhance employment opportnities for students, especially from rural and underprivilleged background.
