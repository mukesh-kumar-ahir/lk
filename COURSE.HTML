COPA :- COMPUTER OPERATOR AND PROGRAMMING ASSISTANT

COPA stands for Computer Operator and Programming Assistant. It is an ITI treade that teaches students computer operation, programming, enternet usege, office applications, database management, and basic software development. Students learn skills such as MS Office, HTML,CSS,JavaScript,C/C++,Python,data entry ,and computer networking. After completing COPA, students can work as computer operators , data entry operators, office assistants, programmers, or pursue higher studies in the IT field.
